# JF Hub

## Business Management System

## Vision
JF Hub is a private business management platform designed to centralize the operation and analysis of multiple businesses from one simple and fast interface.

The first business modules are:

- CHISPEKAS — inflatable games, snack carts and event services.
- Racks — products, sales, costs and profitability.

## Philosophy

> One Hub. All Your Business.

JF Hub is not intended to be a complex ERP. It is a practical decision tool.

Every feature must satisfy at least one principle:

1. Save time.
2. Help make better decisions.
3. Increase business visibility.

## What JF Hub is not

- Not a customer portal.
- Not a payment platform.
- Not a replacement for Google Calendar.
- Not a complicated accounting system.
- Not a system full of unused modules.

## Development principles

- Mobile first.
- Simple interface.
- Fast access to important information.
- Data before decoration.
- Features only when they provide real value.

## Future direction

JF Hub should become a personal business command center where information from all businesses can be analyzed quickly and intelligently.
